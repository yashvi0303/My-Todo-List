Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

It may have one or more beds, but the size of the bed depends on the hotel. Some single rooms have a twin bed, most will have a double, few will have a queen bed.


Family Room

the room is larger than the standard room and will accommodate 4- 6 people, usually with an extra twin bed or sleeper sofa.


Duplex Room

A duplex room or duplex suite is a room which is split into two levels. Often, the bottom floor will be a more common area, with a kitchenette and a living room or lounge, while the top floor may be a loft space or mezzanine with just a bed or a full bedroom.
